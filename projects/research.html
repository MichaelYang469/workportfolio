XAIGID-RewardBench: Explainable AI-Generated Image Detection

First-author research benchmark for evaluating whether multimodal LLMs can judge explanation quality in AI-generated image detection tasks.

Problem

AI image detectors need explanations, not just labels.

AI-generated image detection is moving beyond simple real/fake classification toward systems that explain why an image appears authentic or generated. Multimodal LLMs can act as policy models that produce explanations and reward models that judge which explanations are stronger.

XAIGID-RewardBench evaluates how well current MLLMs perform as reward models for this task, identifying which models can reliably judge explanation quality and where they still fall short.
